Char&Stands

Jonathan Joestar

Jonathan is the first Jojo that we meet and he's perhaps the most boring. He is a gentleman and just a nice guy which is part of why we root for him because Dio essentially ruins his life for no good reason. He starts off as a rather timid weakling but over the course of Phantom Blood we watch him grow into a much stronger and confident character as he masters Hamon to defeat Dio

Joseph Joestar

Joseph is almost the opposite of Jonathan. While Jonathan was a gentleman, Joseph is more of a swindler and will do anything to win fights. He often uses trickery to win fights rather than just using strength. When he fights he is always planning ahead and will often call out what his opponent will do next. In Stardust Crusaders we see his stand, Hermit Purple. Hermit Purple allows him to control vines to attack and defend although he rarely uses it in battle. It also has the ability to let him see things from far away using cameras or other objects. Joseph is featured in parts 2, 3, and 4.

Jotaro Kujo

Jotaro is probably the most iconic Jojo. He is essentially the strong and silent type with him rarely speaking or saying what he is thinking but he is the strongest in the group that travels to Egypt. His stand is Star Platinum which has the ability to punch really fast and really hard. His stand is rather lacking compared to later stands and it makes fights at the beginning of Stardust Crusaders a little bland as Jotaro usually just wins by puching harder than his opponent although as things continue we see Jotaro fighting enemies he can't defeat through pure strenght and we see him start to strategize. Jotaro appears in parts 3, 4, 5, and 6 making him the JoJo we see the most throughout the series.

Josuke Higashikata

Josuke is a highschooler and unlike Jotaro he actually acts like one. He has a lot of personality and is very sensitive about his hairstyle to the point that if anyone mocks it he will go into a blind rage. His stand is Crazy Diamond and it has the power to fix things alhtough he can't use it to heal himself. He uses this ability in a lot of interesting ways that don't just involve healing people. One of my favorite uses of his power is when he breaks a window and turns the shards into projectiles by fixing it.

Giorno Giovanna

Giorno is probably most notable for his goal to become a Gang Star and his morals. He grew up with an abusive stepfather but his life changed one day when saves the life of a gangster. After that he was never abused again he started to see that the mafia had real power in the city so he desires to become a part of that mafia to make the city a better place. His stand is Gold Experience which has the power to turn inanimate objects into living things. This power is used in a lot of different ways like how he can heal people by turning rocks into body parts. Also at the beginning of Golden Experience, the things he brings to life will reflect any damage taken but then that ability just disappears a few episodes in with no explanation.
