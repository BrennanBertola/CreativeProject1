Author

 Hirohiko Araki first Debuted back in 1980 with a wild west one-shot known as Poker Under Arms. It wasn’t until 6 years later in 1986 when the first volume of JoJo’s Bizarre Adventure was released. Since then Araki has gone on to finish 8 seperate parts of JoJo’s resulting a whopping 957 total chapters over the course of 35 years. After 35 years everyone was expecting the now 61 year old Araki to announce that part 8 would be where the JoJo's franchise finally meets its end. It’s not difficult to imagine the shock when he instead came out to announce Part 9 currently named JOJOLANDS. It seems that he fully intends to write this story into his twilight years and we are all here for it.

Brennan Bertola & Ben Giles
